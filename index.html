Welcome to my world!

I am an AI engineer at vinci4d.ai and a Ph.D graduate in Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering from Rutgers University, advised by Prof. Y.B. Guo. My research lies at the intersection of physics-informed machine learning (PINN) and metal additive manufacturing — building deep learning frameworks grounded in governing physical laws to predict thermal fields, melt pool dynamics, stress evolution, and fatigue failure in laser powder bed fusion processes.

I also serve as an ML intern at Intelligent Twins, developing State-of-the-Art ML models and physics-informed solvers for different clients.

Prior to my Ph.D., I spent three years as a Simulation Engineer at Caterpillar Inc., after my MS from IIT Kanpur. My work was focused on structural integrity analyses for engine-mounted components.

Feel free to reach out for collaborations or opportunities.
